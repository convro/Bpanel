Domains

Connect your domains to this server. Point your domain's A record to your server IP, then add it here.

How to connect a domain

  1. Go to your domain registrar (Cloudflare, Namecheap, GoDaddy, etc.)
  2. Find DNS settings for your domain
  3. Add an A record pointing to your server IP: loading...
  4. Wait for DNS propagation (usually 5-30 min, max 48h)
  5. Come back here and click "Add Domain"
  6. Optionally add SSL certificate in the SSL tab

Requirements
